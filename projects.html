Visual-Inertial SLAM on the Northeastern Autonomous Car

For this project, as part of the Robotic Sensing and Navigation course at Northeastern, my team and I integrated the ORB-SLAM3 algorithm with ROS for localization and mapping on the NUance autonomous car using Stereo Visual-Inertial SLAM.

Virginia Tech Baja SAE

The Skunkworks sub-team designed an electronically-controlled limited-slip differential for the Baja off-road vehicle. As the Electronic Systems lead, I designed the control system and selected and integrated the hardware in addition to implementing our proposed system in simulation (CarSim).

Final Report

Computer-Vision Tracking Eyes

This project was an extension of a Servo Smoothing experimental robot designed by James Bruton. Using a webcam and Raspberry Pi, OpenCV was used to track my face and control the eyes to follow my movements.
